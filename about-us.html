ABOUT US
WHY ARE WE DOING THIS?

Hey there! Remember those days in polytechnic when you'd eagerly queue up for the 3D printer, only to end up with subpar prints and wasted time? Yeah, been there, done that. It was beyond frustrating, having to stick around just to keep an eye on the machine, hoping your project wouldn't be the next victim of poor quality.

That's why we started WE ARE GRIND*R, to help out other students facing the same struggles we did. As former students ourselves, we understand the frustration of unreliable 3D printing services.

WHAT WE OFFER

Welcome to WE ARE GRIND*R, where we're dedicated to empowering student creativity through 3D printing innovation. We offer affordable pricing plans and exclusive educational discounts to make our services accessible to all. Our customizable student packages provide everything you need for your projects, from design assistance to flexible printing options, ensuring you can bring your ideas to life on your schedule.

With convenient online ordering and delivery to campus locations, we make it easy for you to access our services anytime, anywhere.
